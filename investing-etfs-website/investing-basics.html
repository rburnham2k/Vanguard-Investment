Investing Basics

Learn the fundamentals of investing and how Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) fit into a smart investment strategy.

Key Concepts

  • Diversification: Spreading investments across various assets to reduce risk.
  • Risk Tolerance: Understanding how much risk you can withstand based on your financial goals.
  • Time Horizon: The amount of time you have to invest before needing the money.
  • Cost Averages: How expense ratios and fees impact long-term returns.

How ETFs Work

ETFs are investment funds that trade on stock exchanges, combining the diversification of mutual funds with the flexibility of individual stocks.
